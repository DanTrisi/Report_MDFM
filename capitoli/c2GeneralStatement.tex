\section{General statement of the client’s dissatisfaction}
The machines currently available on the market require the constant presence of an operator, 
are not very flexible, and are relatively slow in operation.

The machines currently available on the market are relatively slow in processing a log in input into the final chunk; not very flexible in terms of allowed log diameters in input (there is little possibility to change the diameter once the machine is switched on), and finally they need the presence of an operator in order for them to perform the process. 

So, our goal is to try to fix these issues; in particular, the core of the project will be focused on automatizing the entire firewood processor and finding a solution that allows to adjust the working log diameter in real time.
